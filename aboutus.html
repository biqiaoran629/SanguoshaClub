"Sangox'a Club was founded in 2010 by Jack Liu. Over 5 years, we have over 500 members registered and we are still growing. Our weekly events happen each Friday from 5pm - 10 pm during school terms, and you will know the location of events if you leave your name in the Join Us page, you will receive our weekly flyer. We will be offering drinks, crackers and all kinds of boardgames not only Sangox'a, but also Avalon, Dixit, etc. We've also hosted 4 major events: Two Sangox'a Contests, and two "If You Are The One" events that are similar to a famous Chinese show. All of these are for only $10/year. Feel free to join us!
